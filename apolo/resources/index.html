    Solv Protocol and NFT Financialisation

    Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) have experienced unprecedented growth, traction, and value creation over the past two years, with ‘blue chip’ collections such as Bored Ape Yacht Club and Crypto Punks becoming well known and desired by the crypto native community. In their simplest form, NFTs provide verifiable authenticity, ownership, and scarcity over a distinct, digitally-native good or asset. For the most part, these properties are facilitated by the popular ERC-721 standard, an open standard that describes how to build NFTs on EVM (Ethereum Virtual Machine) compatible blockchains.

    The Solv Protocol and NFT Financialisation Report covers the new wave of NFT financialisation broken down into two distinct categories:

    • The use of NFT token standards in Decentralised Finance (DeFi); and
    • Using NFTs in DeFi to enhance liquidity, utility, and yield.
